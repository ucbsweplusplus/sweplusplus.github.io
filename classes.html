About Us

Piloted under the UC Berkeley chapter, this organization holds a ten-week programming course that encourages the development of a female computer science community and teaches students the foundations of Python. Currently our classes are held for middle school girls (6th to 8th grade) .

Our classes start with an introduction to computer science priciples through Sratch. We introduce the basics of logical thinking and get introduce the general terms like “conditional” and “variable”. We spend the rest of the time learning python. We spend each classes talking about certain topic starting with variables and types of objects (int, float, etc) and ramp up as the week go hitting things such as conditionals, for loops, while loops, and lists (among others).
