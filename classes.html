What We Do

Piloted under the UC Berkeley chapter, this organization holds a ten-week programming course that encourages the development of a female computer science community and teaches students the foundations of Python.


How to Join

Currently our classes are held for middle school girls (6th to 8th grade). Our applications for the Spring 2019 semester are currently closed. Check back early next fall to apply to be a part of out Fall 2019 cohort!
