What is Code Day?

Code Day is our final event of the semester where we invite girls from all over the area to participate in a day filled with fun engineering workshops and activities.

This is a day to make new friends, learn more about computer science and women in tech, and learn about careers in CS. Last semester, we had workshops on arduinos, building your own website, and other engineering activities like marshmallow and spaghetti towers!

Who Can Join

This event is open to middle school girls in the area. You do not have to be a part of the SWE++ classes to participate! No coding experience necessary!
